About


SciSky is owned and operated by Skylar Hoffert.


I started this adventure in January 2018 in an effort to share my passion for space and science with others. I want SciSky to be more than a YouTube channel -- I want to build SciSky into a community. Having discussions and thinking deep on our place in the Universe will help us feel a little more connected to the Cosmos. We all need to realize how wonderful the Cosmos is, and how fortunate we are to be a part of it!
